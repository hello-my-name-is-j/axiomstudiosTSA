About

At Axiom Studios, our goal is to bring high quality virtual experiences at an affordable price. We've been working since 2002 to make VR and AR products more accessible, while still maintaining their quality. We offer a range of products, from the affordable Axi-S Parallel to the proffesional Axi-S Cube. We also create AR apps, usually for education, such as the Axi-S Branch.
