What is VR?

VR, or Virtual Reality, is a cutting edge technology that allows users to feel fully immersed in other worlds. Whenever we look at something, our brain actually sees two subtly different images, due to our eyes being offset. Our brain can combine these two images and give us a sense of depth. VR uses this to trick our brain by displaying two slightly different images directly in front of our eyes. Our brain then interprets this as depth, and the user feels like they are in the world displayed to them.

What is AR?

AR is an initialism for Augmented Reality. While VR generates and beams an image to your eyes, AR is a filter that is placed on top of what you are already seeing. Imagine a world where you can paint in the air or test different room designs; the possiblities are only limited by imagination.
