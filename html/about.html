About

At Axiom Studios, our goal is to bring high quality virtual experiences at an affordable price. We've been working since 2002 to make VR and AR products more accessible, while still maintaining their quality. We offer a range of products, from the affordable Axi-S Parallel to the professional Axi-S Cube. We also create AR apps, usually for education, such as the Axi-S Branch. Our prices are guaranteed low because of our intuitive design and being able to produce material efficiently. Our process is proprietary, making sure no material is wasted during the process. All our manufacturing is done in America and made in the state of California, where we can ensure successful processing.
